Tracey and the Magic brush (WIP)

Description:

Tracey is an aspiring, young artist who was invited by an art festival to display her art for the first time.
However, disaster strikes as somone steals her paintings the day before the festival!

Help Tracey use her artistic skills and elemental colors to draw the path to recovering what's hers!

(The game can even be controlled with a Wii Remote!)

Windows macOS
