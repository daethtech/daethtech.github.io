Tracey and the Magic brush (WIP)

Description:

Tracey is an aspiring, young artist who was invited by an art festival to display her art for the first time.
However, disaster strikes as she bumps into a petty witch who traps her in her own paintings on her way to the festival!

Help Tracey use her artistic skills and elemental colors to draw the path to find her way out and to the festival!

(The game can even be controlled with a Wii Remote!)
